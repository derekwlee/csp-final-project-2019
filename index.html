What Are Seasonal Allergies?

As human beings, our immune systems have evolved to be able to detect harmful substances in our bodies. However, there are times when our immune systems turn the tables against us and reacts towards harmless substances, such as pollen, bee venom, pet dander, and foods. Seasonal allergies, also known as hay fever, specifically occur when a person's immune system detects the increased pollen levels in the air.
